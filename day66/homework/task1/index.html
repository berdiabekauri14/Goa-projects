Getting somthing on console

  1. You have to right click on the empty space in the browser
  2. You have to choose "inspect"
  3. After that you have to choose "Console"

To get somthing in the console you must use console.log('text')

But if you want to have Two texts in the console you must use ";" in the end, "example: console.log("text1"); console.log("text2")"
